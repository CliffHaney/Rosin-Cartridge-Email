Fill your jar with rosin! Hash rosin will absolutely perform much better than flower rosin. We recommend to not fill the jar over the halfway mark to allow for headspace in the jar. This prevents any over flowing/boiling of oil from the jar.

Pre heat your oven to 150f. Yes, you can use a vacuum oven, but it's not necessary. The more accurate your oven, the better.

Place your sealed jar into the oven for 24-48 hours. Sometimes it can take a little bit longer, depending on how much material there is and how stable the material was to begin with.
DO NOT OPEN UNTIL YOU ARE READY TO FILL YOUR CARTS!
Every time you open a hot jar of hash, you're releasing volatile terpenes.

Pull your jar when you have a water like viscosity with very few rising bubbles. If you have more bubbles than what's shown in this video, put it back in the oven. Put your glass syringe in the oven to keep it warm.

Fill your warm glass syringe with the decarbed Rosin.
 

Flip it vertically, and slowly insert and pull the syringe in and out. This will "smash" the bubbles on the top layer of the oil.

Fill your cartridges! Don't worry about filling the bottom of the cartridge, we'll wick them in a moment.

Wick your cartridges! Lower the temperature in your oven to about 100f. Put the cartridges in the oven for about 5 minutes. This melts the oil down into the cartridge, and soaks the wick for enjoyable first hits.
We do not recommend putting foam in the oven, however this polystyrene is safe up to over 400f/204c

Top off each cartridge again with your syringe. Do not overfill.

Cap your cartridge and enjoy! It's really that easy.
